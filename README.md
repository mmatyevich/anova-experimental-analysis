## Overview
This project applies ANOVA to assess group differences in a synthetic dataset based on previous data, with a focus on interpreting results in applied research settings.

## Methods
- One-way and repeated measures ANOVA
- Post-hoc comparisons

## Key Insights
- Identified statistically significant differences between experimental conditions
- Interpreted group effects in relation to study design and expected outcomes
- Demonstrated how statistical testing informs conclusions in experimental research

## Tools
R (aov, tidyverse)

## Relevance
Demonstrates core statistical techniques used in analysing experimental and health-related data, including interpretation of results to support research conclusions
